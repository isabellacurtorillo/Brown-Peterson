Proctor Instructions

Before continuing to the test, a control panel will appear on the screen. This panel will allow you to control the parameters of the test. The control panel will ask you for:

  • Total Number of trials
  • Distractor task quantity 1
  • Distractor task quantity 2
  • Distractor task quantity 3

Once these metrics are set, the "Start Task" button will appear. Click this button to begin the task.
